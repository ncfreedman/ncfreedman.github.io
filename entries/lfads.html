Latent Factor Analysis via Dynamical Systems (LFADS)

lfads addresses the issue of extracting latent factors from single trials. essentially introducing a new way to extract latent factors which at its core learns the dynamics of the network. after training, the model estimates an initial condition and then generates the dynamics of the latent factors using a recurrent network paired with a feedforward model. how does it work? how was it validated? comments on using kinematic decoding as a way of comparing different dimensionality reduction methods
