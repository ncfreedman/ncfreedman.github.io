Hello! I am an MD PHD student in the University of Pittsburgh & Carnegie Mellon University MSTP focusing on neuropsychiatry, computational neuroscience and machine learning. I am interested in using computational methods to study information processing in the brain and to reveal how these processes are altered in psychiatric disease. I believe that working to ground our theories of psychiatric illness in neural computation will help guide effective therapies.

This Fall, I'll begin my PhD in the Program in Neural Computation at CMU. I will be working with Steve Chase, Aaron Batista & Byron Yu to study mechanisms of learning and memory in the nonhuman primate neocortex using brain computer interfaces.
