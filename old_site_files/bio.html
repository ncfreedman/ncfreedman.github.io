bio

I am a MD PhD student in the University of Pittsburgh & Carnegie Mellon University MSTP interested in connections between neural dynamics, human cognition and psychiatric disease. I am motivated to develop computational frameworks to better interpret neural signals, understand basic mechanisms of the brain and ultimately yield improved diagnostic methods and interventions in the clinic. Contributing to the design of more effective therapies on the basis of neurocomputational theory is one of my major goals.
