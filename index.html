I am a MD PhD student in the University of Pittsburgh & Carnegie Mellon University MSTP. My scientific interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, neural dynamics, human cognition and psychiatric disease. I am motivated to develop new methods of analysis to better interpret neural signals in the brain, elucidating basic computational mechanisms and how they are altered in mental conditions. A long-term vision of mine is to accelerate the integration of algorithms in the psychiatric clinic to aid in improved diagnosis and in the design of more successful interventions.
