I am a MD PhD student in the University of Pittsburgh & Carnegie Mellon University MSTP. My scientific interests lie at the intersection of applied mathematics, machine learning, neural dynamics, human cognition and psychiatric disease. I am motivated to develop new methods of analysis for better interpreting neural signals in hopes of elucidating basic computational mechanisms of the brain. A long-term vision of mine is to leverage computational methods for improved diagnosis and design of interventions in the clinic.
