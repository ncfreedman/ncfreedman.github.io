I am a 1st year MD PhD student in the University of Pittsburgh & Carnegie Mellon University MSTP. My interests lie at the intersection of neural dynamics, human cognition and psychiatric disease. I am motivated to develop new methods of analysis for better interpreting neural signals in hopes of elucidating basic computational mechanisms of the brain and with a long-term vision of improving diagnostic methods and interventional approaches in the clinic.
